## Features Summary

**Kasir Baik** is a complete POS application featuring: Point of Sale with barcode scanning & table live view, cash drawer management, credit/debt management, stock & inventory control with purchase orders & multi-unit products, expense tracking, 20+ comprehensive reports (sales, cashflow, P&L, balance sheet, capital, tax, inventory, product performance, customer, staff, payment methods, hourly sales, supplier, credit, debt, cash drawer, purchase order, stock history, stock adjustment), master data management (products, categories, variants, units, customers, suppliers, tables, POS categories, cash reasons, order statuses, vouchers, users), Bluetooth thermal printer support (58mm/80mm), data export (Excel & Google Sheets), app lock & Google Sign-in security, multi-user roles, freemium/premium subscription model, store settings (logo, tax, currency, QRIS, bank account), local & cloud backup with history, multi-language support, first-time onboarding, factory reset, and responsive design for phones & tablets.
